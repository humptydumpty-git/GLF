Frequently Asked Questions

How are my donations used?

Your donations directly fund our programs in education, disaster relief, healthcare, and community development. We ensure that at least 85% of all donations go directly to program implementation.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Gamers Lounge Foundation is a registered charity. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Absolutely! You can choose "Monthly Giving" to set up automatic recurring donations. This provides stable funding for our ongoing programs.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use Paystack for secure payment processing. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers. We're also PCI compliant.
