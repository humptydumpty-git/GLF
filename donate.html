Frequently asked questions

How are donations used?

Gifts support our charitable work as described in our mission—gaming and e-sports for social impact, inclusive events, wellbeing-focused programmes, and youth opportunity. We use funds responsibly and can answer questions about how donations are applied.

How do I get a receipt?

After a successful payment, keep your transaction reference from Paystack. For formal or tax receipts, email us with your details and we will help where applicable under local rules.

Can I donate more than once?

Yes. You can return to this page anytime to make another one-time donation.

Is my payment information secure?

Payments are processed by Paystack. Card and payment details are handled on their secure pages; we do not store full card data on this website.
