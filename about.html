About GLF

Who we are, what we believe, and what we are building—straight from our vision and mission.

Who we are

Gamers Lounge Foundation (GLF) is a registered charitable organisation founded by people who believe gaming can be a powerful, positive force. Our work is centred on gaming and e-sports as tools for social impact, especially for orphans, young people, and underserved communities.

We run inclusive programmes and events that combine play with connection, care, and practical support. Meet the people behind GLF and explore programmes & activities.

Our vision

To build a world where gaming becomes a powerful force for social impact—transforming lives, fostering connection, and improving the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of underserved and vulnerable communities.

Mission

What we commit to do

1. Social impact through gaming

To leverage gaming and e-sports as tools to positively impact lives by creating engaging platforms that bring joy, hope, and meaningful experiences to orphans, the less privileged, and underserved individuals.

2. Holistic community support

To organise inclusive gaming events that integrate health screenings, emotional support, and social connection, fostering an atmosphere of love, care, and fellowship for vulnerable populations.

3. Youth empowerment & digital inclusion

To empower young people by promoting digital skills, creativity, and opportunities within the gaming ecosystem, enabling them to build sustainable and meaningful futures.

4. Safe & inclusive spaces

To create safe, welcoming environments where individuals from all backgrounds can connect, collaborate, compete, and grow through shared gaming experiences.

5. Love-driven service

To demonstrate compassion through practical acts of love, giving selflessly to uplift lives and inspire lasting change within communities.
