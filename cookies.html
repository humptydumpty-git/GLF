Cookie Notice

Last updated: March 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites work, remember preferences, or understand how visitors use the site.

How we use cookies

Depending on how this site is configured, we or our service providers may use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies required for basic functions (for example security or load balancing).
  • Functional cookies that remember choices you make.
  • Analytics cookies that help us measure traffic and improve content (only if you enable such tools).

Third parties

Embedded content or payment, form, or chat tools (for example payment gateways or form providers) may set their own cookies. Their policies govern those cookies.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings (block, delete, or alert). Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the site work.

Where required by law (for example UK/EU), we will obtain consent before non-essential cookies and document your choices.
