Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Gamers Lounge Foundation (“GLF”, “we”, “us”) is a charitable organization. This policy explains how we handle personal data when you use our website, contact us, donate, or interact with our services.

Information we may collect

  • Contact details you provide (for example name, email, phone, message content) when you use our contact form or other channels.
  • Donation-related data processed by our payment provider (for example transaction references). We do not store full card numbers on our web servers.
  • Technical data such as browser type, general location (country/region), and pages visited, if you or our tools collect it for security or analytics.

How we use information

We use personal data to respond to enquiries, process donations, operate and improve our website, comply with law, and protect against fraud or abuse.

Legal bases (UK / EEA)

Where UK GDPR or EU GDPR applies, we rely on appropriate grounds such as: your consent (where required), performance of a contract or steps at your request, our legitimate interests (for example responding to messages and securing our site), and legal obligations.

Sharing and processors

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us (for example form delivery, payment processing, or hosting). They process data only on our instructions and under appropriate safeguards.

International transfers

If data is transferred outside your country, we take steps that are appropriate under applicable law (for example standard contractual clauses where required).

Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above, including legal, accounting, and reporting requirements.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, object to certain processing, or request portability. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights, contact us at gamersloungefoundation@gmail.com.

Children

Our site is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have collected a child’s data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.
