Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GLF

How are donations used?

Donations support our charitable programmes—gaming and e-sports for social impact, inclusive community events, and youth opportunity. We use funds responsibly and can answer questions about how gifts are applied.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Tax treatment depends on your country and our registration status where you file. We can provide a payment or donation acknowledgement where applicable—please email us and consult a tax adviser for your situation.

How can I volunteer with GLF?

We welcome volunteers. Email us or message on WhatsApp to say you’re interested—we’ll share opportunities that match our mission (events, outreach, digital skills, and community support).

Can I donate items instead of money?

In-kind support may be possible depending on current needs. Please contact us before sending items so we can confirm what we can receive and use well.

How do I start a fundraising campaign for GLF?

We'd love to help you fundraise! Contact us with your idea and we'll provide you with materials, guidance, and support. You can organize events, birthday fundraisers, gaming tournaments, or any creative idea you have.

How can my company/organization partner with GLF?

We welcome corporate partnerships! Email us with “Partnership” in the subject line and our team will reach out to discuss sponsorship opportunities, matching gifts, employee engagement programs, and more.

How do I report an issue or provide feedback?

We take feedback seriously. Email us or use WhatsApp to report any issues or share your suggestions. We’re committed to continuous improvement.

What areas does GLF work in?

We are based in Ghana, West Africa. Our mission is gaming and e-sports for social impact—community connection, wellbeing, and youth inclusion. Partnerships may extend where they align with that purpose.
