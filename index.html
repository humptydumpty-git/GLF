Gaming that lifts communities

Gamers Lounge Foundation (GLF) uses gaming and e-sports to bring joy, connection, and practical support to orphans, young people, and underserved communities—through inclusive events, wellbeing-focused programmes, and digital inclusion.

Vision & focus

We believe gaming can be a serious force for good: building connection, confidence, and care for people who need it most. Everything on this site reflects that purpose—no exaggeration, just what we stand for and what we are working toward.
